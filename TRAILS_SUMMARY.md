# Trails Summary

> Generated by `scripts/write_summaries.py` — do not edit by hand; re-run `make data` after changing the bounding box.

The map overlays a baked trail layer (`public/trails.geojson`) rendered as named lines with along-line labels and a toggle. Trails are ingested as GeoParquet, split into <=1 km pieces, and densified to LineString Z (a DEM elevation sample ~every 10 m).

## Source

- **Dataset:** [Overture Maps](https://overturemaps.org/) — transportation theme, `segment` type
- **Release:** `2026-05-20.0`
- **Derived from:** OpenStreetMap (ODbL — © Overture Maps / OpenStreetMap contributors)
- **Project bounding box:** lon `-118.8288 … -118.2699`, lat `36.2682 … 36.7708`
- **Filter:** `subtype = road` and `class ∈ {path, footway, track, steps, bridleway, pedestrian}` with a non-null name
